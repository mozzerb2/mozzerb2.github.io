Software as a Service

We will run our transparent mathematical model, which yields the optimal bid-ask placement, on your exchange. We do what you’re supposed to do, ethically, and charge a fixed fee. This eliminates any incentive to manipulate the market.

We have spent the last 3 years developing multiple high frequency trading strategies for the crypto market and are proud to announce that we have achieved consistent results with a Sharpe ratio of greater than 4. Cyber security & privacy

In an ever evolving data-driven world, it can be difficult to keep up with the rapid pace of technology. Our team of cyber security experts are here to advise you on how best to secure your digital assets and ensure that your infrastructure has commensurate security controls in place.

Privacy is a global issue, with a heightened sense of regulatory and public awareness, meaning privacy compliance is expected at all times. Our team of privacy experts have a broad understanding of the privacy risks and areas of operational improvement from legal, compliance, technology, and data governance perspectives.

Data science

Organisations are amassing vast volumes of data from online transactions, IoT devices, systems and people. It is imperative to collate this data as it holds many insights about your operations, performance, customers and competitors. Our team of experienced data scientists is here to unlock this potential and maximise the value of your datasets.

We will provide you with our capability for a full implementation of data-driven projects, from strategy to deployment, enabling you to unleash the power of your data and drive business decisions.
