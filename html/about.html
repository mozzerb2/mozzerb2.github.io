The crypto-sphere is undergoing a revolutionary transition

Within the next few years, we firmly envisage blockchains and digital currencies having a managed and regulated role in the global economy, which will be achieved by removing intermediaries. Founded in 2019, Cryptosphira is a technology company with a focus on providing professional services for blockchains and digital assets. We strongly believe that valuing security and privacy are paramount to achieving a successful decentralised ecosystem and we will not forsake them in our service ambition.

Our values

Our mutual desire to achieving prosperity through decentralisation is driven by our core values

Canonize Security & privacy

Our customer’s security and privacy are of paramount importance to us, which is why we won’t collect any data that is not absolutely vital for our services.

Build Trust

We want to establish an open dichotomous relationship with our customers, where trust is at the epicentre and clear communication is imperative.

Remunerate the Community

We believe that a healthy ecosystem is created and sustained by giving back to the community through knowledge-sharing and collaboration.
